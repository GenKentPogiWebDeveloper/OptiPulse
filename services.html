VISION

To establish OptiPulse as a trusted academic platform that simplifies and enhances students' online learning experiences, promoting better productivity and organization..

MISSION

To provide students with a reliable and accessible tool that integrates academic resources, helping them stay focused, organized, and efficient in managing their academic responsibilities.

GOALS

1. Launch OptiPulse within the set timeline and onboard at least 40-50 users within the first 19 days.
2. Collect user feedback to identify areas for improvement and refine the platform accordingly.
3. Demonstrate the platform's effectiveness during live presentations or through faculty and peer endorsements.
4. Develop basic but functional features like the integrated app hub, notifications, and personal notes for immediate usability.
